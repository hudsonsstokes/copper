# copper
🚀 **Live Demo:** [https://copper-training--stokeshudson.replit.app](https://copper-training--stokeshudson.replit.app)

An intelligent, interactive AI workout trainer designed to personalize and optimize your fitness routine.

## What It Does
Copper acts as your personal digital fitness coach. It leverages advanced AI to analyze your fitness goals, track your progress, adapt routines in real-time, and provide interactive form guidance to ensure safe and effective workouts.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React / Next.js
- **AI Engine:** Chat GPT API
- **Backend:** Node.js / Express
- **Deployment:** Replit
